What We Do?

We do domestic and commercial renovations. This includes modifying existing buildings; additions and alterations; revamping without any civil work changes and any similar renovation work.

Development of a building from ground up, covering land surveying; foundation building, all the way to roofing. We will also do your electrical; plumbing and gas and solar installation.

We provide consultation solution in carrying out civil; plumbing; gas and electrical building assessment prior to a sale or purchase of a property. Nkwally Developments will provide a detailed technical report, post the assessment, to help you make an informed decision about the sale or purchase of a property.

Nkwally Developments also supports with regular household and office space maintenance. Here we do regular interval inspection of general maintenance items of your property in as far as your civil; electrical; gas and plumbing systems of your property.

At Nkwally Developments we also specialize in helping you creatively design your house from the brick and mortar to how you would like your daily interior space to look. We also help to make your work and living space, energy and cost efficient.
