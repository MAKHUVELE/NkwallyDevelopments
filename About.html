About Us

Nkwally Developments is founded on identifying a need in the Southern African construction market for high-quality, compliant, modern and cost-efficient projects.

This is done with the customers’ idea and concept of the construction project as the central focus. We deliver to reality projects that display the values, ideas and wishes of the inceptor, the customer who mandated it.

At the end of every project, Nkwally Developments strives to leave a product that is over satisfactory to the client.

Vision

  • We envision Nkwally Developments as a trusted, responsible and sustainable building and construction company in Africa.
  • A go-to partner for turnkey project solutions across construction, civil and commercial & residential maintenance.

Mission

Daily quality; professional and trustworthy workmanship in Africa.
Providing seamless execution of construction projects.
Completing resilient projects that minimise environmental impact and promote energy efficiency.
